The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pokémon: The First Movie

"Pokémon: The First Movie" is a beloved classic in the world of 'Pokemon,' capturing the essence of adventure, friendship, and the enduring spirit of Pikachu and his fellow pocket monsters.

Pokémon 3: Spell of the Unown

"Pokémon 3: Spell of the Unown" is the third installment in the Pokémon movie series and follows the adventures of Ash, Pikachu, and their friends as they encounter the mysterious and powerful Unown, leading to a spellbinding and emotional journey in the Pokémon world.

I Choose You!

"I choose you!" is a classic phrase from the Pokémon series, often used by trainers when selecting their Pokémon to battle. It's a moment filled with excitement and anticipation as trainers send their chosen Pokémon into the arena.
